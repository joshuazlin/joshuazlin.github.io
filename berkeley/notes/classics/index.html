Classics 36: Greek Philosophy

Random number: 99

Notes on the Classics 36 course taught in Fall 2016 by Professor Magrin.

Routes:

Chapter 1: Xenophanes on the nature of the gods

Chapter 2: Parmenides' challenge and Democritus' answer

Chapter 3: Socrates in the Apology: virtue as the key to happiness vs. other goods such as wealth and honor

Chapter 4: Socrates in the Meno: the difference between knowledge and opinion

Chapter 5: The Greater Hippias: why Plato posits Forms and what is a Form

Chapter 6: Aristotle’s Ethics: the definition of happiness; virtues of character and of thought

Chapter 7: Aristotle’s Physics: natures vs. forms (natural things are like “the snub”)

Chapter 8: Aristotle’ On the Soul: the definition of soul and the faculties of the soul

Chapter 9: Epicurus’ physics: the gods and providence

Chapter 10: Epicurus’ ethics: the goal of life

Chapter 11: Stoic physics: the first principles and their relation (LS)

Chapter 12: Stoic ethics: virtue (LS) and choice (Epictetus)
