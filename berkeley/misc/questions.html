Chaos
Define formally what we mean by chaos; e.g. what exactly do we mean when we say that the double pendulum is chaotic as opposed to the single pendulum? I'm almost certain that there should be many different equivalent ways of characterising the 'chaotic' condition.

Quantum Trading Information
Is it possible to come up with an algorithm that utilises qubits (and probably entanglement?) such that Alice and Bob can 'trade' information under the following parameters:
  • At the moment that Alice becomes able to retrieve Bob's information, Bob must be able to retrieve Alice's information (and vice versa). In other words; one party can't 'back out' of the deal; and just steal the other party's information.
  • The protocol is secure; in the sense that any eveasdropper listening in cannot recover anything about either Alice's information or Bob's information.
    • Classically, this is impossible. In the Quantum world; we have entanglement on our side, so maybe it's possible; though I'm hedging my bet that it's actually impossible. A good start maybe would be to get Alice and Bob to share a secret key (with BB84 or the like)? Or maybe utilise quantum teleportation somehow.
